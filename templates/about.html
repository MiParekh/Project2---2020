About the Project

We have created a superhero database which will help you get to know the profiles for superheroes from various publishers. We’ll also look into their characteristics, powers, and stats. Through an interactive analytics dashboard that includes a sunburst, scatter plot, radial bar chart, and table of all heroes, we can find information about many of your favorite heroes. We’ll look at data over for over 700 superheroes and we’ll also find out how each of them can help solve the world’s most difficult problems. We also aim to find out who are the superheroes who can fictionally save the Earth by identifying 167 super abilities that exist among beings across the universe which can potentially assist with one or more current critical world issues which are (Medical (COVID - 19), Climate Change, Drug Addiction, Educational Improvements, Infrastructure Improvements, Large Scale Conflict).
