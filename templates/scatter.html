Correlation of Superhero Powerstats

There are moderate to strong correlations between all general attributes. Among all the relationships, the three highest correlations were: Strength/Durability (0.79), Combat/Intelligence (0.78), and Power/Durability (0.75). Conversely, the weakest correlations are Strength/Intelligence (.57), Strength/Combat (.58), and Speed/Combat (.59).

While a scatterplot is helpful to see the correlations between general attributes and scores for high level assessment, a deeper dive into individual superpowers is needed to thoroughly understand which superheroes can help the most. For example, are there any superpowers that lead to a strong combination of durability and strength which can help with a real world issue such as COVID-19.
