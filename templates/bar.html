Top Superheroes

Analysis

The top superheroes with highest total power statistics were further analyzed.

The average total score for the top 150 Superheroes was 434 with median of 421, ranging from 356 to 581 (Martian Manhunter).

There are 24 superheroes and villains had a total score of 500 or more.

The number of superheroes and villains with a 100+ score in a general attribute: Intelligence(16), Strength(24) Speed (10), Durability(48), Power(49), Combat(10).

A total of 94 or 63% of the top 150 are good superheroes as shown by blue markers.

Durability is the attribute with the highest average score with mean of 86.6 and median of 90, while Speed is the lowest with mean score of 52.8 and median of 51.

While a bar chart is helpful to see the which superheroes are the most versatile and their total rating, a deeper dive into individual superpowers is needed to thoroughly understand which superheroes can help the most. For example, 48 superheroes had a durability rating of 100+, but is there any specific superpower that can help with a real world issue such as COVID-19.
